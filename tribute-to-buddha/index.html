Gautam Buddha

The Enlightened One

  • 563 BCE

    - Birth of Siddhartha Gautama in Lumbini (present-day Nepal).

  • 547 BCE

    - Marriage to Yasodhara; lives a royal life in Kapilvastu.

  • 534 BCE

    - Renounces worldly life after seeing the Four Sights (old age, sickness, death, ascetic). Leaves palace to seek enlightenment.

  • 534-528 BCE

    - Practices severe asceticism and meditation in search of truth.

  • 528 BCE

    - Attains Enlightenment under the Bodhi tree in Bodh Gaya; becomes the Buddha ("the Enlightened One").

  • 528-483 BCE

    - spends the rest of his life teaching the Dharma (Buddhist teachings) across India.

  • 483 BCE

    - Parinirvana (death) at Kushinagar; leaves the cycle of rebirth.
